## Slide 2 — The Challenge (Why Partner?)

**Title:**  
**The Challenge: Faster Awareness, Better Response**

**Bullet points (keep short):**
- Citizens see hazards and incidents first—but often have no direct channel to DRRMO.
- Barangay-level data is scattered (paper, phone calls, social media), slowing city-wide situational awareness.
- DRRMO needs **real-time visibility** into fires, accidents, medical emergencies, and environmental issues across barangays.
- Building a single, city-aligned reporting platform from scratch is costly and time-consuming.

**Closing line:**  
*What if barangays and the city could share one modern platform for incident intake and real-time tracking?*

**Visual:**  
- Simple graphic: citizens → ??? → DRRMO (current) vs. citizens → B-SAFE → DRRMO (proposed).

## Slide 3 — The Solution: B-SAFE

**Title:**  
**B-SAFE: One Platform for Residents and Officials**

**One-sentence pitch:**  
B-SAFE is a modern web platform where **residents submit incident reports** (with photos/videos and location) and **officials—including DRRMO—triage, investigate, and respond** with real-time updates.

**Short bullets:**
- **Resident side:** Simple report form, evidence upload, status tracking.
- **Official side:** Command center with dashboard, reports list, analytics, and live updates.
- **Pilot:** Barangay Banicain, Olongapo City (locations and report types already configured).
- **Ready to scale** to other barangays and to align with DRRMO workflows.

**Visual:**  
- Screenshot or mockup of the resident form + one of the official dashboard.

## Slide 5 — Key Features (What DRRMO Gets)

**Title:**  
**What B-SAFE Delivers**

**Bullets (4–6):**
1. **Real-time intake** — Reports appear instantly; no waiting for paper or phone logs.
2. **Evidence at submission** — Photos and videos attached by residents for better assessment.
3. **Live status stream** — Officials see new/updated reports in real time (server-sent events).
4. **Unified dashboard** — One place to see all reports, priorities, and status (Seen → Processing → Done).
5. **Analytics-ready** — Trends, hotspots, and KPIs to support planning and reporting.
6. **Transparent tracking** — Residents see progress; builds trust and reduces duplicate reports.

**Visual:**  
- Small icons per bullet or one dashboard screenshot with callouts.

## Slide 6 — Key Benefits of Accepting B-SAFE as a Partner

**Title:**  
**Why DRRMO Olongapo Benefits from This Partnership**

**Subtitle:**  
Key benefits of accepting B-SAFE as an official partner

**Numbered or card-style list:**

1. **Stronger situational awareness**  
   - One channel for barangay-level incidents (fire, accident, medical, environmental) that DRRMO can monitor and use for response and reporting.

2. **Faster response**  
   - Reports and evidence reach officials immediately; no manual consolidation from multiple sources.

3. **Better data for planning and reporting**  
   - Structured, timestamped reports and analytics support LGU reports, DRRM plans, and funding justifications.

4. **Cost-effective solution**  
   - Use an existing, piloted platform instead of building and maintaining a separate system.

5. **Scalable across Olongapo**  
   - Start with Barangay Banicain; roll out to more barangays with the same platform and workflows.

6. **Aligned with citizen engagement**  
   - Residents report once; barangay and city (DRRMO) benefit—improves trust and compliance with DRRM initiatives.

7. **Modern, secure foundation**  
   - Web-based, role-based access; ready to integrate with existing DRRMO processes and future systems.

**Visual:**  
- One icon per benefit or a simple “partnership benefits” graphic.

## Slide 7 — How the Partnership Could Work

**Title:**  
**How the Partnership Works**

**Flow (simple diagram or bullets):**
- **Residents** (Banicain, later other barangays) submit reports via B-SAFE (web).
- **Barangay officials** triage and update status in the same platform.
- **DRRMO** (as partner) gets:
  - **Option A:** Access to the same dashboard (view/all or filtered by type/barangay).
  - **Option B:** Regular feeds or exports (e.g., incident summary by day/week) for DRRMO’s own systems.
  - **Option C:** Dedicated “DRRMO view” (e.g., only hazard/emergency categories).
- **Data use:** Incident data supports DRRMO’s mandate (awareness, response, reporting, planning) under agreed data-sharing and privacy guidelines.

**Visual:**  
- Simple flow: Residents → B-SAFE → Barangay + DRRMO.

## Tips for the Actual PPT
